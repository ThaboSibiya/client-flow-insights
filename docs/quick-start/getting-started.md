
# Getting Started with Quikle CRM

Welcome to Quikle CRM! This guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## 🎯 What is Quikle CRM?

Quikle CRM is a comprehensive customer relationship management system designed for South African businesses. It helps you:
- Manage customer information and relationships
- Track sales pipelines and support tickets
- Automate workflows and communications
- Generate quotes and invoices
- Analyze business performance

## 📝 First Steps

### 1. Logging In
1. Navigate to your Quikle CRM URL
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"
4. You'll be redirected to the Dashboard

### 2. Understanding the Interface

#### Main Navigation (Left Sidebar)
- **Dashboard** - Overview of your business metrics
- **Customers** - Customer database and management
- **Conversations** - All customer communications
- **Employees** - Team management (Admin only)
- **Pipeline** - Sales and support workflows
- **Quotes & Invoices** - Financial document management
- **Analytics** - Business performance reports
- **Onboarding** - New user setup
- **Audit Log** - Security tracking (Admin only)

#### Top Bar
- **Quikle Logo** - Returns to Dashboard
- **User Profile** - Account settings and logout

### 3. Your First Tasks

#### For New Users:
1. **Complete your profile** - Click on your name in the bottom left
2. **Explore the Dashboard** - Get familiar with the overview
3. **Add your first customer** - Go to Customers → Add Customer
4. **Set up email integration** - Visit Pipeline → Settings → Email

#### For Administrators:
1. **Add team members** - Go to Employees → Add Employee
2. **Configure email settings** - Pipeline → Settings → Email
3. **Set up automation** - Pipeline → Automation
4. **Review security settings** - Audit Log

## ⚠️ Important Notes

- **Data is automatically saved** - No need to manually save changes
- **Real-time updates** - Changes appear immediately for all users
- **Mobile responsive** - Works on phones and tablets
- **Secure by default** - All data is encrypted and protected

## 💡 Quick Tips

- Use the search function to quickly find customers or tickets
- Drag and drop items in the pipeline to update status
- Click on any metric card in the Dashboard for detailed views
- Use keyboard shortcuts: Ctrl+/ for search
