
# Customers - Your Customer Database

The Customers page is where you manage all customer information, track interactions, and maintain relationships.

## 🎯 What the Customers Page Does

Manage your complete customer lifecycle:
- **Store customer information** - Contact details, preferences, history
- **Track interactions** - Calls, emails, meetings, tickets
- **Monitor pipeline status** - Where customers are in your sales process
- **Generate reports** - Customer analytics and insights
- **Bulk operations** - Efficient mass updates

## 📝 Page Layout and Features

### 1. Top Action Bar
**Left Side:**
- **Add Customer** button - Create new customer records
- **Import** button - Bulk import from CSV/Excel
- **Export** button - Download customer data

**Right Side:**
- **Search box** - Find customers quickly
- **Filter** button - Advanced filtering options
- **View options** - Table or card view

### 2. Filter Panel (Expandable)
Quick filters for finding customers:
- **Status** - Active, Inactive, Prospect, Customer
- **Date Added** - When customer was created
- **Assigned To** - Team member responsible
- **Pipeline Stage** - Current sales stage
- **Tags** - Custom labels
- **Location** - Geographic filters

### 3. Customer List/Table
Main customer display with columns:
- **Name** - Customer full name
- **Company** - Business name (if applicable)
- **Email** - Primary contact email
- **Phone** - Primary phone number
- **Status** - Current customer status
- **Pipeline Stage** - Sales progress
- **Last Contact** - Most recent interaction
- **Assigned To** - Responsible team member
- **Actions** - Quick action buttons

### 4. Bulk Actions (When Items Selected)
When you select multiple customers:
- **Change Status** - Update multiple statuses
- **Assign to Team Member** - Bulk assignment
- **Add Tags** - Apply labels to multiple customers
- **Export Selected** - Download specific customers
- **Delete** - Remove multiple customers (Admin only)

## ⚠️ Key Customer Management Features

### Adding New Customers
1. Click **"Add Customer"** button
2. Fill in required information:
   - Name (required)
   - Email (required)
   - Phone number
   - Company name
   - Address
   - Notes
3. Set initial status and pipeline stage
4. Assign to team member
5. Click **"Save"**

### Customer Profile Details
Click on any customer name to view their complete profile:

**Contact Information Tab:**
- Personal details
- Multiple phone numbers
- Email addresses
- Physical address
- Emergency contacts

**Interaction History Tab:**
- All conversations and calls
- Meeting notes
- Email history
- Timeline of activities

**Tickets Tab:**
- Support requests
- Issue tracking
- Resolution history
- Satisfaction ratings

**Financial Tab:**
- Quotes and invoices
- Payment history
- Outstanding balances
- Revenue generated

**Notes Tab:**
- Internal team notes
- Customer preferences
- Special instructions
- Follow-up reminders

### Customer Status Management
Track customer progress through your business process:

**Status Options:**
- **Prospect** - Potential customer
- **Lead** - Qualified prospect
- **Customer** - Active paying customer
- **Inactive** - Former customer
- **Blocked** - Restricted access

**Pipeline Stages:**
- **New Lead** - Just discovered
- **Contacted** - Initial outreach made
- **Qualified** - Meets criteria
- **Proposal** - Quote/proposal sent
- **Negotiation** - Discussing terms
- **Closed Won** - Became customer
- **Closed Lost** - Did not convert

## 💡 Customer Management Best Practices

### Daily Tasks
1. **Review new customers** - Check recent additions
2. **Update interaction notes** - Record all customer contact
3. **Follow up on overdue** - Check follow-up reminders
4. **Move pipeline stages** - Update customer progress

### Weekly Tasks
1. **Clean up data** - Remove duplicates, update info
2. **Review assignments** - Ensure proper team member assignment
3. **Analyze conversion** - Check pipeline performance
4. **Plan outreach** - Schedule customer communications

### Monthly Tasks
1. **Export reports** - Download customer analytics
2. **Review inactive customers** - Re-engagement campaigns
3. **Update customer segmentation** - Refine targeting
4. **Training updates** - Share best practices with team

### Data Quality Tips
- **Always verify email addresses** - Prevents bounce-backs
- **Use consistent naming** - Standard format for companies
- **Add detailed notes** - Help team members understand context
- **Update regularly** - Keep contact information current
- **Use tags effectively** - Organize customers logically
