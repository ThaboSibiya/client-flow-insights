
# Quotes & Invoices - Financial Document Management

The Quotes & Invoices page is your complete financial document center for creating, managing, and tracking all customer quotes and invoicing.

## 🎯 What the Quotes & Invoices Page Does

Complete financial document management:
- **Create professional quotes** - Branded proposals for customers
- **Convert quotes to invoices** - Seamless sales-to-billing workflow
- **Track payment status** - Monitor outstanding amounts
- **Automate follow-ups** - Scheduled reminders and communications
- **Generate financial reports** - Revenue and cash flow analysis

## 📝 Page Layout and Features

### 1. Document List View
**Filter and Search Options:**
- **Document type** - Quotes, Invoices, or All
- **Status filter** - Draft, Sent, Approved, Paid, Overdue
- **Date range** - Creation or due date filtering
- **Customer filter** - Search by customer name
- **Amount range** - Filter by document value
- **Team member** - Filter by document creator

**Document Cards Display:**
- **Document number** - Unique identifier (QUO-001, INV-001)
- **Customer name** - Who the document is for
- **Document type** - Quote or Invoice indicator
- **Amount** - Total value prominently displayed
- **Status badge** - Current document status
- **Due date** - Payment or expiry date
- **Actions menu** - Edit, send, duplicate, delete options

### 2. Document Creation Form
**Basic Information:**
- **Document type** - Quote or Invoice selection
- **Customer selection** - Choose from existing customers
- **Issue date** - Document creation date
- **Due date** - Payment deadline (invoices)
- **Valid until** - Quote expiration date
- **Subject line** - Brief description of document

**Line Items Section:**
- **Description** - Detailed service/product description
- **Quantity** - Number of items/hours
- **Rate** - Price per unit
- **Amount** - Calculated total per line
- **Add/Remove** - Dynamic line item management

**Calculations Panel:**
- **Subtotal** - Sum of all line items
- **Discount** - Percentage or fixed amount reduction
- **Tax** - VAT calculation (South African rates)
- **Total** - Final amount due

### 3. Document Preview and Actions
**Preview Features:**
- **Professional layout** - Branded document appearance
- **Company logo** - Your business branding
- **Complete details** - All document information formatted
- **Terms and conditions** - Payment terms and policies
- **Contact information** - Your business contact details

**Action Options:**
- **Send via email** - Direct customer delivery
- **Download PDF** - Local file storage
- **Print document** - Physical copy generation
- **Share link** - Secure online viewing
- **Duplicate** - Create similar documents quickly

## ⚠️ Key Financial Features

### Quote Management
**Quote Creation Process:**
1. **Select customer** - Choose recipient from database
2. **Add line items** - Detail all services/products
3. **Apply discounts** - Negotiate pricing as needed
4. **Set expiry date** - Quote validity period
5. **Review and send** - Final check before delivery

**Quote Status Tracking:**
- **Draft** - Work in progress, not sent
- **Sent** - Delivered to customer
- **Viewed** - Customer opened the quote
- **Accepted** - Customer approved the quote
- **Rejected** - Customer declined the quote
- **Expired** - Quote validity period passed

### Invoice Management
**Invoice Creation:**
- **Convert from quote** - Seamless quote-to-invoice workflow
- **Create new invoice** - Direct invoice generation
- **Recurring invoices** - Automated regular billing
- **Progress invoicing** - Partial payment invoicing

**Payment Tracking:**
- **Unpaid** - Outstanding customer payments
- **Partially paid** - Partial payment received
- **Paid** - Full payment completed
- **Overdue** - Past due date payments
- **Refunded** - Payment returned to customer

### Automation Features
**Email Automation:**
- **Quote follow-ups** - Automatic reminder emails
- **Payment reminders** - Overdue invoice notifications
- **Thank you messages** - Payment confirmation emails
- **Renewal notices** - Subscription or service reminders

**Workflow Automation:**
- **Quote expiry notifications** - Alert before expiration
- **Auto-convert to invoice** - When quotes are accepted
- **Overdue escalation** - Progressive collection process
- **Payment confirmation** - Automatic status updates
