
# Pipeline - Sales & Support Workflows

The Pipeline page is where you manage your sales process and support ticket workflows, tracking progress from initial contact to completion.

## 🎯 What the Pipeline Page Does

Manage your complete business workflows:
- **Visual workflow management** - Drag-and-drop kanban boards
- **Sales pipeline tracking** - Lead to customer conversion process
- **Support ticket management** - Issue resolution workflows
- **Team performance monitoring** - Track progress and bottlenecks
- **Automation setup** - Streamline repetitive processes

## 📝 Pipeline Types and Layouts

### 1. Customer Sales Pipeline
**Pipeline Stages (Default):**
- **New Lead** - Fresh prospects and inquiries
- **Contacted** - Initial outreach completed
- **Qualified** - Meets your ideal customer criteria
- **Proposal Sent** - Quote or proposal delivered
- **Negotiation** - Discussing terms and pricing
- **Closed Won** - Successfully converted to customer
- **Closed Lost** - Did not convert, with reason

**Stage Management:**
- **Add stages** - Create custom workflow steps
- **Edit stages** - Modify stage names and requirements
- **Set stage rules** - Define progression criteria
- **Color coding** - Visual organization by priority/type

### 2. Support Ticket Pipeline
**Ticket Stages:**
- **New** - Recently created tickets
- **Assigned** - Allocated to team member
- **In Progress** - Actively being worked on
- **Pending** - Waiting for customer response
- **Resolved** - Issue fixed, awaiting confirmation
- **Closed** - Ticket completed successfully

**Priority Levels:**
- **Urgent** - Critical issues affecting operations
- **High** - Important issues requiring quick resolution
- **Medium** - Standard support requests
- **Low** - General inquiries and minor issues

## ⚠️ Key Pipeline Features

### Card Information
Each pipeline card displays:
- **Customer/Ticket name** - Clear identification
- **Value/Priority** - Sales value or urgency level
- **Assigned team member** - Responsible person
- **Days in stage** - Time tracking for bottleneck identification
- **Last activity** - Recent actions or communications
- **Tags** - Custom labels for organization
- **Quick actions** - Call, email, or edit options

### Drag-and-Drop Functionality
**Moving Items:**
- **Drag cards** - Move between stages easily
- **Bulk selection** - Move multiple items at once
- **Automatic updates** - Status changes trigger notifications
- **Activity logging** - Track all movements and changes

**Stage Rules:**
- **Required fields** - Information needed before progression
- **Approval workflows** - Manager approval for certain stages
- **Automation triggers** - Actions when items move stages
- **Time limits** - SLA requirements for ticket resolution

### Filtering and Views
**Filter Options:**
- **Assigned to me** - Show only your items
- **By team member** - Filter by responsible person
- **By date range** - Specific time periods
- **By value range** - Sales pipeline value filters
- **By priority** - Support ticket urgency levels
- **By tags** - Custom label filtering

**View Options:**
- **Kanban board** - Visual drag-and-drop interface
- **List view** - Table format with sorting options
- **Calendar view** - Timeline-based visualization
- **Analytics view** - Performance metrics and reports

## 💡 Pipeline Management Best Practices

### Daily Pipeline Review
**Morning Routine:**
1. **Check high-priority items** - Address urgent tickets first
2. **Review your assigned items** - Plan your daily activities
3. **Update item status** - Move items to appropriate stages
4. **Add activity notes** - Document progress and next steps
5. **Schedule follow-ups** - Set reminders for future actions

**End-of-Day Review:**
1. **Update progress** - Move items based on day's work
2. **Add internal notes** - Document important information
3. **Plan tomorrow** - Prioritize next day's activities
4. **Update time tracking** - Record time spent on items

### Sales Pipeline Management
**Lead Qualification:**
- **BANT criteria** - Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline
- **Scoring system** - Rate leads based on conversion probability
- **Source tracking** - Monitor lead generation effectiveness
- **Follow-up scheduling** - Consistent prospect communication

**Opportunity Management:**
- **Value estimation** - Realistic sales projections
- **Probability weighting** - Likelihood of closing deals
- **Competitor tracking** - Monitor competitive situations
- **Decision maker identification** - Key contact mapping

### Support Ticket Management
**Ticket Triage:**
- **Priority assignment** - Based on business impact
- **Skills-based routing** - Match tickets to expertise
- **SLA monitoring** - Track resolution time commitments
- **Escalation procedures** - Move critical issues appropriately

**Resolution Tracking:**
- **Time logging** - Accurate work time recording
- **Solution documentation** - Build knowledge base
- **Customer satisfaction** - Measure resolution quality
- **Root cause analysis** - Prevent recurring issues
