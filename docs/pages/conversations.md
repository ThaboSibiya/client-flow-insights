
# Conversations - Communication Hub

The Conversations page is your central communication center where all customer interactions are managed and tracked.

## 🎯 What the Conversations Page Does

Your complete communication management system:
- **Centralize communications** - All customer messages in one place
- **Track conversation history** - Complete interaction timeline
- **Manage team responses** - Assign and collaborate on customer communications
- **Monitor response times** - Track team performance
- **Organize by priority** - Focus on urgent communications first

## 📝 Page Layout and Features

### 1. Conversations List (Left Panel)
**Filter and Search Bar:**
- **Search box** - Find conversations by customer name or content
- **Status filter** - All, Active, Pending, Resolved
- **Priority filter** - High, Medium, Low
- **Assigned filter** - Filter by team member
- **Date range** - Filter by conversation date

**Conversation Cards:**
Each conversation shows:
- **Customer name** - Who you're communicating with
- **Last message preview** - Quick content overview
- **Timestamp** - When last message was sent
- **Status indicator** - Active, pending, or resolved
- **Priority badge** - Urgency level
- **Unread count** - New messages indicator
- **Assigned team member** - Who's responsible

### 2. Message Thread (Right Panel)
**Conversation Header:**
- **Customer information** - Name, contact details
- **Conversation actions** - Mark resolved, assign, set priority
- **Quick actions** - Call customer, create ticket, send quote

**Message History:**
- **Chronological messages** - Complete conversation timeline
- **Message types** - Email, SMS, internal notes
- **Sender identification** - Clear team member vs customer messages
- **Timestamps** - Exact send/receive times
- **Read receipts** - Message delivery status
- **Attachments** - Files and images in messages

**Message Composer:**
- **Rich text editor** - Format messages professionally
- **Attachment upload** - Add files and images
- **Template selector** - Use pre-written responses
- **Internal notes** - Team-only comments
- **Send options** - Email, SMS, or internal

## 💡 Best Practices for Customer Communication

### Daily Routine
1. **Check high-priority conversations** - Address urgent issues first
2. **Review assigned conversations** - Handle your assigned communications
3. **Update conversation status** - Keep status current
4. **Add internal notes** - Document important information
5. **Set follow-up reminders** - Schedule future communications

### Response Guidelines
**Professional Communication:**
- **Use proper greetings** - "Good morning [Customer Name]"
- **Be clear and concise** - Easy-to-understand language
- **Stay positive** - Helpful and solution-focused tone
- **Proper closing** - "Best regards" with your name and contact

**Response Time Targets:**
- **High priority** - Within 1 hour
- **Medium priority** - Within 4 hours
- **Low priority** - Within 24 hours
- **Follow-up** - Within 48 hours if no customer response

### Team Collaboration
**Internal Notes Best Practices:**
- **Document key information** - Important customer details
- **Share context** - Background for team members
- **Update on progress** - Status of issue resolution
- **Handoff information** - When transferring conversations

**Assignment Guidelines:**
- **Assign by expertise** - Match skills to customer needs
- **Consider workload** - Balance team member assignments
- **Include context** - Explain why assigning to specific person
- **Set expectations** - Timeline for response or resolution
